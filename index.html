How it works

Plug-in / SDK layer enables compliant player-to-player trading, keeping studios in control of fees and rules.

1) Integrate

Integrate the SDK/API and define what can be traded (in-game currencies and/or items).

2) Verification & compliance

Identity verification (KYC/KYB), age verification, and AML (anti-money laundering) screening are built into the transaction flow, together with legal/tax handling.

3) Players trade

Players trade with each other when they want. The studio earns a cut per transaction, with no operational burden.

Players can

Trading is optional and player-initiated. The studio does not buy or sell; it only earns a cut from completed trades.

  • Trade in-game assets with other players.
  • Trade in-game currencies with other players.
  • Pay/receive fiat or crypto (depending on studio settings and requirements).
  • Optional: Convert specific in-game assets into NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) and trade them.

Make trading official and tracable

Unofficial player-to-player markets already exist across the majority of games. Trades are difficult to monitor and typically occur off-platform. Our solution formalizes in-game trading within an official tracable flow.

  • Replace grey markets: move activity on-platform instead of off-site.
  • Increase visibility: track transactions and fee revenue.
  • Reduce scams and fraud: verified identities lower impersonation and social-engineering attacks.
  • Limit chargebacks: structured flows and verification reduce payment disputes.
  • Protect player accounts: fewer incentives for account theft and resale.
  • Keep studio control: studios define what is tradable and under which rules.
